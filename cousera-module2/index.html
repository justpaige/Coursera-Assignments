Ancestors of the Piano

The Monochord



A single-stringed instrument that is thought to have been used to experiemnt with the relationship between sound and math.

The Harpsichord



A piano-like instrument created in the 16th century. When a key would be pressed, the action would pluck the string.

The Pianoforte



A popular instrument in the 17th century, and very similar to the modern piano, with action that strikes each string/group of strings with a felted hammer.
